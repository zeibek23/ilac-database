About Drugly

Drugly is a cutting-edge platform dedicated to advancing personalized medicine through comprehensive drug data and pharmacogenomic insights via the publicly available datasets around the world.

Our Mission

We aim to empower healthcare professionals, researchers, and clinicians as well as public with accurate, accessible, easy-to-understand, and actionable drug information to improve treatment outcomes and enhance public's awareness about the medications.

Our Vision

To lead the way in precision medicine by integrating genetic data, drug interactions, and advanced analytics into a user-friendly platform.

Our Team

Drugly is built by a passionate team of pharmacologists, data scientists, and software engineers committed to innovation in healthcare. Special thanks to Nurcan for her unwavering dedication throughout this journey.
